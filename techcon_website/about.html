The Origins and Evolution of TechCon

TechCon began in 2005 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts passionate about innovation and collaboration.
Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world, attracting professionals and companies from diverse industries. From its humble beginnings in a community center to occupying global stages, TechCon symbolizes the spirit of technological advancement.

In the last decade, TechCon has pioneered discussions on artificial intelligence, renewable tech, and digital transformation, shaping the future of the industry.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech industry. We aim to connect brilliant minds, inspire groundbreaking ideas, and provide a platform for the technology of tomorrow.

Driven by principles of inclusivity, sustainability, and continuous learning, TechCon serves as a beacon for aspiring technologists and established leaders alike.

Past Speakers

Grace Hopper

A pioneer in computer programming, Grace Hopper's contributions to COBOL and compiler development revolutionized software engineering. Her keynote at TechCon 2010 highlighted the importance of adaptability in tech.

Tim Berners-Lee

The inventor of the World Wide Web, Tim Berners-Lee, shared his vision for a free and open internet during TechCon 2015. His ideas continue to inspire generations of innovators.

Sheryl Sandberg

As the COO of Meta Platforms, Sheryl Sandberg emphasized the role of leadership and inclusion in tech during her TechCon 2018 session.
